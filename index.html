Warpcon is a 3-day convention that brings gamers together to socialize, play games and try out different game systems.

Games ran will include

  • RPGs including - D&D 5e, Cyberpunk & Trail of Cthulu
  • CCGs including - Magic: The Gathering, Pokémon, Digimon & Yu-Gi-Oh!
  • Wargames including - Warhammer and Art de la Guerre
  • We will also be hosting LARPS and a Blood Bowl tournament

Warpcon Tickets

There are three different tickets available for Warpcon, a General Admission ticket, a Card Games pass and Wargames pass. The General Admission ticket is required for everyone attending, the Card Games and Wargames pass are optional and gain admission to the respective events.

Vendors

Vendors are welcome to set up and sell their wares to the attendees at the Devere Hall, near Warpcon reception.

Traders

Companies can rent a table to sell to Warpcon attendees for the low cost of €100. To rent a table, fill out the following Google Form. For more information, please reach out to warpcontrader@gmail.com.

Want to join staff?

Staff at Warpon are an integral part of delivering a great experience to our attendees. Warpcon staff will receive a tshirt and food vouchers. We will be putting out a call for staff at Warpcon soon, so keep an eye on our socials for announcements.

Submit your Campaign or Run an RPG One-Shot

Attendees of Warpcon can send an RPG module (DnD, Cyberpunk etc.) which can be ran by GMs during the convention. If we use your one-shot and get a free ticket for Warpcon.

If you would like to send in an RPG one-shot, please fill out the following Google Form as soon as you can, so we can provide GMs with the relevant materials and ample time to prepare.

If you are interested in running an RPG as a GM, we'll be putting out a call for GMs at a later date. GMs who run 3 or more one-shots, will get their Warpcon tickets for free.
